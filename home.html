Our vision: Developing the stakeholders of the entire healthcare system.

  • Our mission: 7 Beats :
  • 1)Introduction: The Illustration of the fields and the specializations inside the healthcare system.
  • 2)Education: Following up on the scientific developments in the fields of work concerned with the healthcare system.
  • 3) Qualification: The gradation from theoretical studies to the fields of work.
  • 4) Employment: Finding suitable mechanisms and ways to connect graduates with work places.
  • 5) Activation: Working on removing the obstacles found in the healthcare system.
  • 6) Communication: Building up a communication network among the members of the healthcare system.
  • 7) Development: Utilizing the hidden capabilities found in both the healthcare and the educational systems
